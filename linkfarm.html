A short apologia

Back in those days, those ancient days, of ... the 90's!!! there weren't really any good search engines and so there were pages that served as so-called "link farms" that could point internet users in the direction of interesting pages through a curated list.
Of course this died with the rise of good search engines like google, and those would never enshittify right???
So in the meantime, whilst projects like ladybird are still a little way off, heres a modern link farm. I hope you enjoy.
